About Grievance Portal

Grievance Portal is a centralized platform designed to address and manage student complaints efficiently. Our mission is to provide students with a secure, transparent, and user-friendly environment to submit grievances and track their progress within the college.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower students by providing a reliable platform for raising grievances, ensuring accountability, and promoting transparency within the college.

Vision: To become the most trusted and efficient online student grievance management system, recognized for timely resolutions and student satisfaction.

Core Features

  • Submit and track complaints online easily
  • Category-based complaint management for better organization
  • Upload evidence securely for faster resolution
  • Admin dashboard to review, update, and resolve complaints
  • Notifications and status updates to keep students informed
  • Secure authentication to protect student data

How It Works

  • Create an account or log in to the portal
  • Fill in the complaint form with details and upload evidence
  • Submit the complaint and receive a unique complaint ID
  • Track complaint status in real-time via your dashboard
  • Admins review complaints and update status until resolution

Security & Privacy

All student data is encrypted and stored securely. The portal follows strict privacy policies, ensuring that only authorized college personnel can access sensitive information. Student complaints remain confidential throughout the resolution process.
