About Grievance Portal

The Student Grievance Portal is a digital platform designed to make it easier for students to raise their issues in a structured and transparent way. Through this system, students can submit their grievances, categorize them, and track their progress until resolution. At the same time, administrators are provided with tools to review, manage, and resolve complaints efficiently. This portal ensures accountability, faster communication, and a more supportive academic.
The Student Grievance Portal is an online platform developed to give students a formal space to share their concerns in a simple and structured way.
It allows users to submit grievances on issues related to academics, facilities, or campus life, and ensures that every complaint is recorded with full transparency.

Features Grievance Portal

The Grievance Portal is designed to make raising concerns simple and stress-free for students and staff. Whether it’s about academics, facilities, or administration, you can submit your grievance, attach any supporting files, and track its progress in real-time. The portal provides administrators with an easy way to manage, assign, and resolve issues efficiently, ensuring that nothing gets overlooked. With notifications, updates, and feedback options, everyone stays informed throughout the process. Prioritizing transparency, privacy, and smooth communication, the portal makes sure every voice is heard and every concern is taken seriously.
